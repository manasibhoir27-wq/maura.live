Why Kids Need to Unplug from Technology

In today's digital age, children spend an average of 4-6 hours daily on screens. Our digital detox camps provide the perfect solution to help them rediscover the real world.

The Screen Time Challenge

Excessive screen time has been linked to sleep problems, reduced attention spans, social skill deficits, and decreased physical activity in children. Our camps in Virar, Vasai, and Nallasopara offer a safe, structured environment where kids can break free from digital dependency.

Problem: Average child spends more time with screens than in school or with family

Solution: Our digital detox camps replace screen time with adventure, creativity, and social interaction

Our Device-Free Promise

At Maura Wonderlab camps, we maintain a 100% device-free environment. All electronic devices are safely stored upon arrival and returned at the end of the camp. Our engaging activities ensure children don't miss their screens!

We provide emergency contact access for parents through camp staff only
