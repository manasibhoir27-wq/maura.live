Team Building Activities for Children

Our curated selection of group activities designed specifically for developing teamwork and leadership in children

Communication Focus

Blindfold Obstacle Course

One team member is blindfolded while others guide them verbally through an obstacle course. Develops trust and clear communication.

45-60 mins
4-6 kids per team
Problem Solving

Escape Room Challenge

Teams work together to solve puzzles, find clues, and "escape" within a time limit. Enhances collaboration and critical thinking.

60-75 mins
5-7 kids per team
Leadership

Team Treasure Hunt

Navigate through clues spread across the campsite, requiring strategic planning and role delegation among team members.

90-120 mins
6-8 kids per team
Coordination

Human Knot Challenge

Team members stand in a circle, grab random hands, and must untangle themselves without letting go. Builds patience and coordination.

30-45 mins
8-10 kids per team
Creativity

Fort Building Competition

Teams use provided materials to build the most creative and sturdy fort. Encourages planning, resource management, and creative collaboration.

60-90 mins
5-7 kids per team
Trust Building

Trust Fall & Support Games

A series of exercises where children learn to trust their teammates, including guided trust falls and balance challenges.

45-60 mins
