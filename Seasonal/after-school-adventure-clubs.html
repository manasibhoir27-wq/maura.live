A Typical Adventure Day

Our after-school program runs from 3:30 PM to 6:30 PM, Monday through Friday, with flexible drop-in options available.

3:30 PM - Welcome & Snack Time

Children arrive from school, enjoy healthy snacks, and settle in with quiet activities or free play.

4:00 PM - Activity Session 1

First structured activity of the day - STEM experiments, creative arts, or nature exploration based on the weekly theme.

5:00 PM - Outdoor Adventure Time

Sports, team games, obstacle courses, or nature walks to burn energy and develop physical skills.

5:45 PM - Activity Session 2

Second structured activity focusing on different skills - life skills, special projects, or themed workshops.

6:15 PM - Wind Down & Reflection

Group sharing of the day's adventures, mindfulness activities, and preparation for parent pickup.
