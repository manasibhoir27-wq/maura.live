Spring Camp Schedule

A typical day at our Spring Break Camp - packed with fun, learning, and adventure!

Camp Days

Day 1: Camp Orientation & Ice Breakers

8:30 AM
Arrival & Registration

Welcome activities, camp rules, and group assignments

9:30 AM
Ice Breaker Games

Fun activities to help campers get to know each other

11:00 AM
Camp Setup & Tent Training

Learn how to set up tents and organize camping gear

1:00 PM
Lunch & Rest Time

Healthy lunch followed by quiet activities and rest

2:30 PM
Team Building Activities

Cooperative games to build trust and communication

4:30 PM
Wrap-up & Departure

Review of the day, awards, and preparation for next day

Day 2: Nature Exploration Day

A full day dedicated to exploring the natural world around us with guided nature walks, plant identification, and environmental awareness activities.

Highlights: Nature scavenger hunt, bug collection (and release), leaf printing art, and learning about local ecosystems.

Day 3: Science & Discovery Day

Hands-on science experiments that make learning fun! From chemistry to physics, campers will be amazed at what they can create and discover.

Highlights: Volcano eruptions, slime making, solar system models, and basic electricity experiments.

Day 4: Adventure & Survival Skills

Learning practical camping and outdoor skills that build confidence and self-reliance in natural settings.

Highlights: Orienteering with maps and compasses, knot tying, basic first aid, and campfire safety.

Day 5: Creative Arts & Final Celebration

A day of creative expression followed by a celebration of the week's accomplishments with parents invited for the final showcase.

Highlights: Nature crafts, tie-dye T-shirt making, talent show preparation, and final campfire with awards ceremony.
