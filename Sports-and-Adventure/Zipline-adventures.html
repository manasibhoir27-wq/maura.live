Important Note for Parents

All children must wear closed-toe shoes and comfortable clothing. Please inform us of any medical conditions or allergies during booking. Weight restrictions: 20kg minimum, 60kg maximum for zipline activities.

Frequently Asked Questions

We offer zipline adventures for children aged 6-15 years. Our courses are divided by age groups: Sky Hawk (6-8 years), Forest Flyer (9-12 years), and Thunderbolt Challenge (12-15 years). Each course is designed with age-appropriate challenges and safety measures.

Safety is our top priority. We use ISO-certified equipment, conduct daily safety checks, maintain a 1:6 instructor-to-child ratio, and all our staff are trained in first aid and emergency procedures. Every child receives proper safety training before their zipline adventure.

Comfortable, weather-appropriate clothing is recommended. Closed-toe shoes (sneakers or sports shoes) are mandatory. Loose jewelry should be removed. We provide all necessary safety gear including helmets and harnesses.

Yes! We have designated viewing areas where parents can watch their children safely. For the beginner courses, parents can accompany younger children during the safety briefing. Photography is allowed in designated areas.

Safety comes first. In case of rain, thunderstorms, or high winds, we postpone the activity. You can either reschedule for another date or receive a full refund. We monitor weather conditions closely and will inform you at least 2 hours in advance if we need to reschedule.
