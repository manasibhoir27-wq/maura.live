Our Safety Commitment

Safety is our #1 priority in every aspect of rifle training

Safety Protocols

  • Double Instructor Supervision

    Every session has a minimum of 2 certified instructors present at all times

  • Equipment Safety Checks

    All firearms inspected before and after each use with detailed logs

  • Ear & Eye Protection

    Professional-grade safety gear provided and mandatory for all participants

Instructor Qualifications

  • NRA Certified Instructors

    All instructors hold current National Rifle Association certifications

  • First Aid & CPR Certified

    Emergency response training for all staff members

  • Child Psychology Background

    Understanding age-appropriate teaching methods

The Four Universal Safety Rules

1

Treat every firearm as if it's loaded

2

Never point at anything you don't intend to shoot

3

Keep your finger off the trigger until ready

4

Be sure of your target and what's beyond it
