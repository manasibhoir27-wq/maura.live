What are Survival Commando Challenges?

Our flagship adventure program combines military-style training with wilderness survival skills, designed to build physical fitness, mental resilience, and teamwork in a safe, controlled environment.

Program Philosophy

At Maura Wonderlab, we believe that controlled challenges help children develop confidence, problem-solving skills, and resilience. Our Survival Commando Challenges are carefully designed by child psychologists and adventure experts to provide the perfect balance of thrill and safety.

Commando Challenge Activities

12+ thrilling activities designed to test limits, build skills, and create unforgettable adventures.

Survival Skills Training

Beginner

Wilderness Navigation

Learn to read maps, use a compass, and navigate through natural terrain using landmarks and celestial navigation techniques.

45 mins Group Activity
Intermediate

Fire Building Techniques

Master multiple fire-starting methods including friction fire, flint and steel, and safe fire management for cooking and warmth.

60 mins Pairs & Solo
Advanced

Water Purification

Learn to identify water sources and purification methods including boiling, filtration, and solar disinfection for safe drinking water.

50 mins Group Activity

Obstacle & Fitness Challenges

Beginner

Commando Crawl Course

Navigate through tunnels, under nets, and over barriers in a timed challenge that builds agility and spatial awareness.

Wall Climbing & Rappelling

Scale vertical walls and learn controlled descent techniques with full safety gear and professional supervision.

75 mins Pairs Activity

Monkey Bridge Crossing
