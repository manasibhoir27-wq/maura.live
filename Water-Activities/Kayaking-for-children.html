Our Safety Commitment

Your child's safety is our top priority. We maintain the highest safety standards in all our kayaking programs.

Safety Protocols & Measures

Certified Instructors

All instructors are certified in CPR, First Aid, and water rescue techniques.

Communication Systems

Two-way radios and emergency communication devices on all kayaking trips.

Weather Monitoring

Real-time weather tracking and strict cancellation policies for unsafe conditions.

Emergency Preparedness

Comprehensive emergency action plans and regular safety drills.

Safety Ratio: 1:6

Maximum of 6 children per certified instructor ensures personalized attention and immediate assistance when needed.

Required for Participation

Completed medical & emergency contact form
Basic swimming ability (floating & treading water)
Parent/guardian consent for children under 12
