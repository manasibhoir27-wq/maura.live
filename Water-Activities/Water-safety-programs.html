Our Safety Commitment

Every water safety program follows strict protocols with certified instructors maintaining a 1:5 instructor-to-child ratio.

  • Certified Instructors: All trainers hold Red Cross or equivalent water safety certifications
  • Safety Equipment: Life jackets, rescue tubes, and first aid kits always on-site
  • Emergency Protocols: Detailed action plans for water emergencies
