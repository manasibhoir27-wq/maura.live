Essential Camping Skills for Youth

Our comprehensive survival skills training program covers everything from basic camping to advanced wilderness survival techniques. Perfect for kids aged 8-16 in the Vasai-Virar region.

Basic

Fire Making & Safety

Learn proper fire-building techniques, fire safety protocols, and how to responsibly manage campfires.

  • Fire starting methods
  • Fire safety rules
  • Extinguishing techniques
Intermediate

Shelter Construction

Build emergency shelters using natural materials and learn about insulation, waterproofing, and site selection.

  • Natural material shelters
  • Tarp setups
  • Site selection principles
Advanced

Wilderness Navigation

Master map reading, compass use, GPS basics, and natural navigation techniques for safe wilderness travel.

  • Map & compass skills
  • GPS basics
  • Natural navigation
Essential

Wilderness First Aid

Learn essential first aid skills for outdoor emergencies, including wound care, splinting, and emergency response.

  • Basic wound care
  • Emergency response
  • First aid kit use
Intermediate

Water Procurement

Learn to find, collect, and purify water in wilderness settings using various filtration and purification methods.

  • Water source identification
  • Purification techniques
  • Safe storage methods
Basic

Essential Knots & Lashings

Master essential camping knots for shelter building, gear securing, and emergency situations.

  • 10 essential knots
  • Lashing techniques
  • Practical applications
