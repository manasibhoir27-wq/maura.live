Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our nature exploration camps for kids.

What age groups are suitable for nature exploration camps?

Our nature exploration camps are designed for children aged 5-16 years. We have specialized programs for different age groups: Little Explorers (5-8 years), Adventure Naturalists (9-12 years), and Wilderness Explorers (13-16 years). Each program is tailored to the developmental needs and interests of that specific age group.

What safety measures are in place during outdoor adventure activities?

Safety is our top priority. All nature exploration activities are supervised by trained naturalists with first aid certification. We maintain a 1:6 staff-to-child ratio, use child-safe equipment, conduct thorough risk assessments of all natural sites, and have emergency protocols in place. Parents receive regular updates during camp sessions.

What should children bring to nature exploration camps?

We provide all specialized equipment. Children should bring: comfortable outdoor clothing, sturdy shoes, hat, sunscreen, water bottle, small backpack, and any personal medication. We'll send a detailed packing list upon registration. All nature exploration tools (magnifying glasses, binoculars, collection jars) are provided by Maura Wonderlab.
