Survival Skills Kids Will Learn

Our outdoor survival camps teach children essential life skills in a fun, engaging environment. From camping skills for youth to emergency preparedness.

Fire Building & Safety

Kids learn safe fire-making techniques using various methods including flint & steel, fire starters, and natural materials.

Water Sourcing & Purification

Finding clean water sources and learning purification methods including boiling, filtering, and chemical treatment.

Shelter Building

Constructing emergency shelters using natural materials, tarps, and basic tools. Learning site selection and insulation.

Wilderness First Aid

Basic first aid training including treating cuts, bites, burns, and recognizing signs of dehydration and hypothermia.

Navigation Skills

Using compasses, reading maps, understanding GPS basics, and natural navigation techniques.

Edible Plant Identification

Learning to identify safe edible plants, berries, and mushrooms while understanding poisonous lookalikes.

Emergency Signaling

Creating signals for rescue using mirrors, smoke, ground signals, and understanding distress call procedures.

Team Survival Challenges

Collaborative problem-solving exercises that teach teamwork, leadership, and communication in survival scenarios.
