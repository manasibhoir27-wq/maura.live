Survival Skills Curriculum

Our comprehensive wilderness survival training covers essential skills every child should know for outdoor safety and confidence.

Ages 8+ Supervised

Fire Safety & Building

Learn safe fire-building techniques using flint & steel, fire safety rules, and proper extinguishing methods in controlled environments.

  • Safe fire pit preparation
  • Alternative fire-starting methods
  • Campfire cooking basics
Ages 7+ GPS Tracked

Navigation & Orienteering

Master map reading, compass use, natural navigation techniques, and GPS basics for safe wilderness exploration.

  • Compass navigation exercises
  • Reading topographic maps
  • Natural direction finding
Ages 6+ Certified

First Aid & Emergency Response

Basic first aid training including bandaging, recognizing emergencies, and calling for help in wilderness situations.

  • Basic wound care & bandaging
  • Emergency communication protocols
  • Recognizing dangerous situations
Ages 9+ Guided

Shelter Building

Construct weather-appropriate shelters using natural materials and basic tools under expert supervision.

  • Natural material identification
  • Emergency shelter construction
  • Tarp and poncho shelters
Ages 10+ Filtered

Water Safety & Purification

Identify safe water sources and learn purification methods for wilderness hydration safety.

  • Water source identification
  • Basic filtration techniques
  • Hydration safety awareness
Ages 11+ Expert-Led

Wilderness Awareness

Develop observation skills, wildlife safety knowledge, and environmental awareness for safe outdoor adventures.

  • Wildlife safety protocols
  • Weather pattern recognition
  • Leave No Trace principles
