Wilderness Adventure Activities

Each activity is designed to be educational, safe, and incredibly fun for children of all ages.

Orienteering & Navigation

Kids learn to read maps, use compasses, and navigate through our specially designed wilderness courses in Nallasopara and Vasai.

Skills Developed:

Spatial awareness, problem-solving, decision-making, and confidence in unfamiliar environments.

Shelter Building

Using natural materials, children construct safe shelters under expert supervision. Teaches resourcefulness and teamwork.

Safety Features:

Plastic tools only, constant supervision, safety gear provided, controlled environment.

Wildlife Tracking

Learn to identify animal tracks, bird calls, and signs of wildlife. Develops observation skills and environmental awareness.

Educational Value:

Biology basics, ecosystem understanding, conservation principles, scientific observation.

Water Safety & Purification

Learn to find and purify water sources safely

Wilderness First Aid

Basic first aid skills for outdoor situations

Outdoor Cooking

Safe cooking methods in wilderness settings

Night Adventure Skills

Navigation and safety during night activities
